About Me

My name is Hamsa Elmi. I am a self-taught Front End Developer in London. I decided to become a web developer because it ensures constant growth and allows me to develop ideas and designs into real websites and web applications allowing me to contribute to the world wide web.


I always had a passion for IT, enjoying lessons in school and I'm now CCNA certified. I first coded HTML in secondary school but was unable to create websites that were not basic and plain. Years later during the first coronavirus lockdown, I began to watch and follow free HTML tutorials on YouTube as a hobby. With those tutorials I became informed of other technologies, such as CSS, that work with HTML to create well designed websites that conform to various devices and screen sizes. From there I decided to pursue web development as a career.


I am currently looking for an opportunity to increase my skills and grow as a developer by being challenged and by having my skills and qualities being put to good use. My current skills are HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
